    AirPods 4 Product Showcase

    Web Development | Design

    An interactive, Apple-inspired 3D product landing page for AirPods 4 that focuses on immersive visual storytelling and premium user experience. The site opens with a full-screen WebGL scene featuring a high-quality 3D model of the AirPods, rendered with realistic lighting, reflections, and smooth animations. Users can interact directly with the product through subtle pointer and scroll interactions, allowing the AirPods to rotate, shift, and respond naturally, closely mimicking the feel of an official Apple product showcase.

    As the user scrolls, the experience transitions into a series of clean, minimal sections that combine motion, typography, and 3D visuals to highlight key features such as active noise cancellation, spatial audio, battery life, and water resistance. Scroll-driven animations guide the narrative, with the product remaining central while text and visual cues fade, slide, or parallax into view. The overall design emphasizes clarity, elegance, and performance, using modern WebGL rendering and smooth interactions to explore how high-end consumer products can be presented on the web in a cinematic and engaging way.
